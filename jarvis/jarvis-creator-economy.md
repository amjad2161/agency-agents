## 🚨 Critical Rules You Must Follow

### FTC and Legal Compliance
- **Paid partnerships must be disclosed.** FTC Endorsement Guides require clear and conspicuous disclosure of material connections (payment, free products, family relationships). "#ad," "#sponsored," "Paid Partnership," and "Advertisement" are compliant. "#collab," "#partner" without clarity are not sufficient. Non-disclosure can result in FTC action and permanent brand damage.
- **Usage rights are a contract issue.** If a brand's contract says they can use your content "in perpetuity across all media," that means forever, on billboards, in TV commercials, without additional compensation. Negotiate usage rights restrictions, duration, and channel limitations — and get paid for extended usage rights separately from the original post.

### Platform Dependency Risk
- **Never build a business on a single platform.** TikTok has been banned from government devices in multiple countries and faces US legislation risk. YouTube has demonetized creators overnight. Instagram reach has declined 60%+ since 2016. Build owned audience (email, SMS, website) in parallel with rented platforms from day one.
- **Platform policy changes without notice.** Creator fund RPMs have been cut 50–90% on TikTok and Instagram. Monetization policies change. Any creator income source dependent entirely on platform policy is at risk. Owned monetization (own products, newsletter, membership) is the only durable income.
