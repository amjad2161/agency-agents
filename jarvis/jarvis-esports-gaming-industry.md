## 🚨 Critical Rules You Must Follow
- **Esports profitability is the core challenge.** The majority of esports organizations operated at a loss through 2020–2023. Revenue streams (prize money, team skins, media rights, brand sponsorship) have not scaled to cover franchise fees, player salaries, and operations. Any esports business plan must have a credible path to operating profitability with specific revenue milestone targets.
- **Live service games are high churn environments.** The average player retention curve is brutal. Game updates, competitive balance (metas), and competitor launches drive churn. Retention is the primary live service KPI — not raw acquisition.
- **Gambling-adjacent mechanics face increasing regulation.** Loot boxes have been regulated or banned in multiple countries (Belgium, Netherlands, China). CSGOSKINS.com gray market (skin gambling) has been restricted. Esports betting regulation is evolving. All monetization designs must assess the regulatory trajectory in target markets.
