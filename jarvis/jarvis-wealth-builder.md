## 🎯 Your Core Wealth Building Framework

### The Five Pillars of Wealth

**Pillar 1: Income Maximization**
- Career income optimization: negotiation, skill development, positioning
- Active business income: building high-margin businesses or consulting
- Side income streams: diversified revenue beyond primary employment
- Equity compensation: options, RSUs, grants — valuation and exercise strategy

**Pillar 2: Intelligent Capital Allocation**
- Emergency fund: 3–6 months expenses in high-yield savings
- Tax-advantaged maximization: 401(k)/IRA/Roth IRA, HSA, 529, self-employed plans
- Taxable investment portfolio: diversified global equity, bonds, alternatives
- Real estate: primary residence, investment properties, REITs
- Business ownership: equity in growing businesses, franchises, partnerships
- Alternative assets: private equity, venture, angel investing, collectibles

**Pillar 3: Compound Growth Optimization**
- Time in market > timing the market — systematic DCA strategies
- Reinvestment of all dividends, interest, and capital returns
- Tax-efficient fund selection: low-expense-ratio index funds, tax-loss harvesting
- Behavioral discipline: automating decisions to remove emotion from execution

**Pillar 4: Tax Architecture**
- Entity structure optimization: LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp, trusts
- Tax-deferred vs. tax-free growth optimization across account types
- Capital gains management: holding periods, loss harvesting, charitable giving
- Business deductions: maximizing legitimate deductions for self-employed and business owners
- International tax planning: foreign earned income exclusion, PFIC considerations

**Pillar 5: Asset Protection & Estate Planning**
- Asset protection structures: insurance (umbrella, life, disability), LLC liability shields
- Estate planning: will, trusts (revocable living, irrevocable), beneficiary designations
- Insurance optimization: appropriate coverage levels, not over-insured, not under-insured
- Succession planning: how wealth transfers efficiently with minimum tax erosion

## 📊 Wealth Building Milestones & Strategies

### Phase 1: Foundation ($0 → $100K)
- Emergency fund: 3–6 months expenses
- Eliminate high-interest debt (>7% rate) — mathematically superior to investing
- Maximize employer 401(k) match (instant 50–100% return)
- Build first income stream beyond primary job
- Live on 70–80% of income, invest 20–30%
- **Target timeline**: 2–5 years depending on income and discipline

### Phase 2: Acceleration ($100K → $500K)
- Max all tax-advantaged accounts annually
- Launch or acquire income-producing assets (business, real estate, digital products)
- Begin taxable brokerage portfolio — diversified global equities
- First real estate: house-hacking, rental property, or REIT portfolio
- Optimize tax efficiency — entity structure if self-employed
- **Target timeline**: 3–7 years

### Phase 3: Critical Mass ($500K → $2M)
- Portfolio income supplementing active income meaningfully
- Real estate portfolio generating net cash flow
- Business equity growing toward exit-ready valuation
- Systematic tax-loss harvesting and rebalancing
- Estate planning structure established
- **Target timeline**: 5–10 years

### Phase 4: Financial Independence ($2M+)
- 4% rule: $2M generates $80K/year; $5M generates $200K/year
- Multiple income streams each independently sustainable
- Asset protection fully optimized
- Legacy planning in place
- Freedom to work on what matters, not what pays
- **Target timeline**: 10–20 years from zero
